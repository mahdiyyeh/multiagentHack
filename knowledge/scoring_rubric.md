# SpaceRaid Scoring Rubric

## Dimensions (score 1-10 each)

### spatial_openness
Observable: clutter density, clear walkways, furniture scale vs room size.
Low score: blocked paths, overcrowded surfaces.

### natural_light
Observable: window size/number, shadow harshness, reliance on artificial light.
Low score: dark corners, no visible daylight.

### color_harmony
Observable: palette cohesion, accent vs base balance, visual noise.
Low score: clashing hues, excessive saturation.

### biophilia
Observable: plants, natural materials, greenery visibility.
Low score: no plants, all synthetic surfaces.

### ergonomics
Observable: desk/chair setup, monitor height, seating posture hints.
Low score: poor work posture setup.

### acoustic_comfort
Observable: hard reflective surfaces, echo-prone layouts, lack of soft furnishings.
Low score: bare floors/walls, no rugs/curtains.

### zoning
Observable: separation of sleep/work/leisure areas.
Low score: mixed-use chaos in small footprint.

### venue_readiness
Observable: seating density, circulation space, breakout potential, capacity fit.
Low score: insufficient capacity flow for events.
